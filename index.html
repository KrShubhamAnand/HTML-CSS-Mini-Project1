Our Work

Introducing -Kr.Shubham Anand

I grew up in Purnea,a city in Bihar,India.I am a passionate photographer who loves to capture & everything that comes with it. I appreciate the people I meet,the places i go,the hours I keep, & the spontaneity in every shoot.


I appreciate the raw reality of everyday life,the fleeting beauty of in-between moments,& I do my very best to take every picture with that in mind.I want my viewers to relate to my photographs through recognition of & familiarity with the situations,the emotions,& the energy so much that they wonder if they are looking at a picture of someone they know, places & situations they have been.
